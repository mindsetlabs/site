About Mike

I'm Mike.
I've lived this.

There was a time I didn't think I'd make it to this point. My mind was a mess — anxiety, depression, and a lot of weight from decisions I had and hadn't made. Not because something was wrong with me, but because I didn't understand what was driving me.

Philosophy and mindset changed that. Now I help others understand themselves and find their way forward.

My work draws from philosophy, coaching, and yoga — but more than anything, it's about creating a space where you can be honest, understood, and find your own way.

What you believe quietly drives everything.
