About Mike

I'm Mike.
I've made the journey back.

There was a time I didn't think I'd make it to this point. My mind was a mess — anxiety, depression, and a lot of weight from decisions I had and hadn't made. Not because something was wrong with me, but because I didn't understand what was driving me.

I found my own way back, then spent years learning how to do it on purpose — in my own life, and in my own body. Philosophy, coaching and movement are how I climbed out, and how I've kept climbing since.

Now that's what I do for others: help people get back from their worst, reach for their best, and understand themselves well enough to keep getting better — for good.

What you believe quietly drives everything.
